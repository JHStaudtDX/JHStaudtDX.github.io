League By-Laws
FREE AGENCY/WAIVERS
$200 FAAB budget, resets at the end of every season only. Payment is due by the time the rookie draft starts (approx 2 weeks after the conclusion of the NFL draft.) $50 per year entry, Inaugural year requires 2 years payment up front. Payout is $450 to 1st place, $100 to 2nd place, $50 to 3rd.
BUY-IN
League safe (majority vote payout) is pinned to Sleeper chat. Owners taking over orphan teams must pay in a minimum of 1 year up front to ensure all owners are invested in the league.
STARTUP DRAFT
Startup draft order determined by derby. I randomize draft order and people select their startup draft slot based on that order. 4 hour draft clock that is paused at 10pm CST and started at 8:00 am CST. Players are still allowed to make picks while the clock is paused.
ROOKIE DRAFT
Rookie drafts will be held approx. 2weeks after the NFL draft concludes. Format: 5 round Snake Seeding: 1-6 toilet bowl, 7-12 inverse of playoff standings.
ROSTERS
Format: 1 QB|2RB|3WR|1TE|2 Flex|1SF Size: 27 including 5 taxi + 2 IR IR + Taxi Rules: See sleeper league settings
LEAGUE CHANGES
League modifications will typically require a majority vote. This includes things like modifying rosters, scoring settings, etc.
TRADES
There is no veto process, please trade responsibly. I will only step in as commissioner if a trade is truly detrimental to the health of the league.
